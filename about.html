Why the name Ekamra

A brand rooted in Odisha's sacred idea of healing and harmony

Ekamra takes its name from Ekamra Kshetra, the ancient sacred landscape of Bhubaneswar, known today as the Temple City of India. For centuries, this region has carried a philosophy of balance, peace and human wellbeing.

  • Ekamra Kshetra: a historic spiritual center associated with Bhubaneswar's temple heritage, including Lingaraj and Mukteswar traditions.
  • Kalinga legacy: Odisha's ancient history reflects resilience, transformation and the pursuit of peace, values that shape Ekamra's patient advocacy.
  • Healing as wholeness: Ekamra's mission is not only to coordinate treatment, but to reduce fear, restore clarity and support families through the emotional work of recovery.
